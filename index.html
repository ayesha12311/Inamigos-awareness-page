About the Foundation

Founded on the Belief That Everyone Deserves a Life of Dignity

InAmigos Foundation was established on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla, with a mission to create meaningful social change across India. Based in Chhattisgarh, the foundation operates as a Section 8 non-profit licensed by the Central Government.

With a dedicated network of volunteers, professionals, and corporate partners, InAmigos works tirelessly to address issues of food insecurity, lack of education, gender inequality, environmental degradation, animal welfare, and unemployment.

Their active social media platforms keep supporters engaged, sharing real-time updates on ongoing campaigns, events, and the community impact being created every day.

Section 8 NGO 80G & 12A CSR-1 Registered NITI Aayog ISO 9001:2015

Mr. Govind Shukla

Founder & CEO, InAmigos Foundation

Driven by a vision of collective action, Mr. Govind Shukla founded InAmigos Foundation to create a platform where every volunteer, donor, and partner could contribute to lasting social transformation.

His leadership has grown the foundation into a nationally recognized organization with registrations in multiple government bodies and partnerships with corporate CSR programs.

Social Impact

Why These Projects Matter

Every project by InAmigos Foundation is rooted in a real community problem. The initiatives collectively address the most pressing challenges faced by vulnerable groups across India — from hunger and illiteracy to gender inequality and environmental damage.

By combining on-ground volunteers, corporate CSR partnerships, and government alignment through NITI Aayog, the foundation ensures that impact is not just visible but sustainable and scalable.

Join as a volunteer, a donor, or simply spread the word — every act of support strengthens communities and creates a ripple of positive change.
